Ham Sandwhich

Description

The most basic of all sandwhiches, but one of the most loved. A great way to relieve your childhood

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Ham
  • Mayonnaise
  • Cheese
  • Lettuce

Steps

  • Take two pieces of bread and coat one side of each evening in Mayonnaise
  • Place one slice of Ham on said Mayonnaised sides of the bread
  • Place one slice of cheese on either of the bread slices
  • Apply a desired amount of fresh Lettuce to middle of sandwhich
  • Stack sandwhich together
